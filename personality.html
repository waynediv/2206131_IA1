Core Traits & Hobbies

Strongest Trait: Flexibility

Flexibility shapes how I adapt to new situations and challenges, especially in my personal and academic life. Whether learning new software tools for editing or adjusting plans for project shifts, my adaptable mindset helps me to stay calm and find effective solutions.

Hobby: Media Editing

Editing videos, music, and other forms of media isn’t just a pastime—it’s a powerful way to express creativity, tell stories, and bring ideas to life. Whether shaping visuals, crafting soundscapes, or blending elements into something unique, it’s about turning vision into reality with passion and precision.

My Hero

Dr. Seuss (Theodor Seuss Geisel) is someone I truly admire—not just for his literary genius but for his ability to think outside the box and challenge conventional ideas. His whimsical storytelling and inventive characters sparked creativity in generations, showing that imagination is a powerful tool for problem-solving and innovation.

"You have brains in your head. You have feet in your shoes. You can steer yourself any direction you choose."

— Dr. Seuss

Personality Test Reflection

According to the Keirsey Temperament Sorter, I am an Idealist. This partially aligns with my personal values, especially my drive to create meaningful and purpose-driven solutions. However, I also lean heavily on analytical thinking, which the test didn’t fully reflect. While such assessments provide useful insights, they can oversimplify our full range of traits.
